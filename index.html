Kite (alpha)

Kite is an opensource replacement to gmail

Host your own webmail, on a server of your own, in almost one click !

Try a demo ! Or

view the screenshots

Based on the interface we all know and love

Google got a lot of things right when they designed gmail. That's why I set out to clone most of the features on gmail in Kite.

As of now, only email reception works, but search, filters and the rest are coming soon !

Screenshots

(Almost) One-click deployment

Kite uses puppet for deployments. This means that deploying a new mail server, including a postfix config with sensible defaults, can be done in seconds !

Built on standards

Kite uses proven opensource tools such as Postfix, Puppet and ElasticSearch.
