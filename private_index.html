Based on the interface we all know and love

Google got a lot of things right when they designed gmail. That's why I set out to clone most of the features on gmail in Kite.

As of now, only email sending and reception work, but search, filters and the rest are coming soon !

Built on standards

Kite is deployable on a Debian/Ubuntu box and uses opensource tools such as Postfix, and ElasticSearch.
